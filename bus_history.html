Team History

The mid 80's was a great time to be a gamer. Consoles were showing up in people's homes at unprecedented rates and we were already a decade into the wonders of "The Oregon Trail". A great time to be alive. It was during this time that the founding member of the n00b_Skoolbus came into being. He seemed a normal child of Humboldt at first, but just one year after his birth, in 1985, something came to America that would change his life forever. The NES.

As soon as he could hold a controller the Nintendo Entertainment System would dominate his life. Long innings of R.B.I. Baseball and endless deaths in Battletoads would forge his soul in red hot 8-bit fires. Although he would not adpot the moniker until years later, by the turn of the century Svenzor had emerged.

Svenzor had earned the heart of a gamer, but one man a team does not make, and it would be years until he would meet his second in command. One day at a small Northern California Community College, history would be made and Death Heim was recruited to the cause. A brutal strategist, Death Heim introduced Svenzor to competative FPS games like Halo: Combat Evolved and Myst.

Since then the n00b_Skoolbus has grown to nearly twice its size, more recently adding the fierce competative Sims 3 phenom RawrImmaZombie and the Bus' resident trolling expert and CS tactician Staberama. To get more specific with the members of our team follow the links below.

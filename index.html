Who are we?

The members of the n00b_Skoolbus are an elite squad of professional video game enthusiasts from the northern region of California, USA. Their ranks swell when new members swear a personal oath and pledge to educate the staggering masses of unknowing fools who populate public servers worldwide. Team nSb actively competes across several genres, most notably FPS games, Minesweeper™ clones, and Freemium titles. They have never officially attended a competition, but are nonetheless feared by many top tier international squads.
